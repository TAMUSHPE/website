    Technical Affairs Committee


    Technical Affairs Committee

    The Tech Affairs Committee is the main source of innovation in TAMU SHPE. We dedicate our time to provide members leadership and technical experience that better prepares them for the engineering industry. As a new member to the committee you are guided through the process of creating your own personal website. This website allows you to provide recruiters an interactive extension of your resume and helps you stand out in a sea of qualified engineers. After successfully creating your website, you are encourage to join (or potentially start your own) a project team with one of our project leads. Some of our projects include Main Website, Python Guide Website, and Personal Website Guide. We are excited to create a better sense of community in the tech affairs committee this year and are excited to have you join. If you're interested in joining, fill out the committee application linked below. Thanks and Gig' Em!
