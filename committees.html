Technical Affairs Committee


Technical Affairs Committee

Our committee is dedicated to all things coding, networking, and data management surrounding SHPE and its national entity. We hope to give our members the necessary skills to code in HTML, CSS, Javascript, and any other language to achieve our goals of technical integration into this great organization. Currently we oversee the automation of the point system, the TAMU SHPE website, and its member database, and are hoping to bring our vision of a TAMU SHPE IOS application in the near future. If you are interested in joining fill out the committee application linked below. Thanks and Gig' Em!

Presidents' Committee


Presidents' Committee

Objective: Establish strong communication with other professional organizations such as SEC, HPC, NSBE, and SWE and the TAMU SHPE Former Students.
Assignments: Work alongside the President and the Former Students Representative to plan joint events with other professional organizations and the Former Students, such as the Alumni Tailgate. Help build and maintain the MemberSHPE Database.
Benefits: You will gain time management and leadership skills while working in a dynamic committee environment. You will get to build your network with other professional organizations and former students.

Internal Affairs Committee


Internal Affairs Committee

Our main objective is to organize and coordinate all athletics, social, and volunteering related events. This committee provides an opportunity to voice your opinions and improve the events we host.
