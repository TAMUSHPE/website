SHPE National Conference

The SHPE National Conference is the major event for the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers, Inc. Additionally, it is the largest technical and career conference for Hispanics in the country.

Each year, the SHPE Conference attracts over 5,000 engineering professionals, students and corporate representatives. The conference is an opportunity for engineering companies and corporations to recruit top talent from SHPE membership. It also provides educational, technical and career opportunities for professional and student engineers.

This year, the SHPE National Conference will be held in Baltimore, MD! Early registration will be open during the summer.

National Institute for Leadership Advancement

NILA

The National Institute for Leadership Advancement (NILA) is the premier leadership training event for SHPE. Since its inception in 1986, NILA has grown to become an event widely anticipated by members and highly respected by corporate sponsors. This year, the conference will take place from Aug 6th to Aug 10th in La Jolla, California.

Regional Leadership Development Conference

RLDC

The Regional Leadership Development Conference (RLDC) is a conference held for individual regions in which chapters from within the same region attend to work on their leadership development skills. It is hosted by different schools each year and held in different locations within the region. More information will be posted as the spring semester approahces.
