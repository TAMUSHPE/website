Entry-to-a-Major

Starting with Fall 2014, freshmen applicants selected for admission are admitted to the Dwight Look College of Engineering with a preference for the major noted on the admissions application. Admitted students follow the first-year engineering curriculum. These changes offer admitted students the freedom to learn about the different engineering majors to better equip them to make informed decisions concerning which engineering discipline is the best fit for their career goals. Acceptance to a major is competitive. All majors are open for applications during each entry-to-a-major cycle.

For updated information check the entry-to-a-major website.
