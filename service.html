ACE Camp

ACE camp is a three day event that takes place at Texas A&M University. We bring high school students with the purpose of encouraging them to seek a higher education in the STEM Field. The students participate in the physics festival, campus tour and engineering challenges with the purpose of developing their leadership skills as well as teamwork and communication skills. In addition, we provide them with information about college applications and financial aid available to them. Application

Noche de Ciencias

Noche de ciencias is an event we have in a high school in the Bryan/ College Station Area. The purpose of this event is to encourage high school students to go to college and seek a career in the STEM field. We set different kinds of activities for the students as well as a presentation to inform them of the opportunities they have.

SHPE Jr. Chapter

Our SHPE Jr Chapter was established during the 2016-2017 academic year and throughout the year we hosted resume workshops, scholarship informational, application for college workshop, successful study tips workshop and end of the year social.

SHPE Jr. Day

SHPE Jr. Day is a one day event in which we bring high schools to Texas A&M University to introduce them to the University and everything it has to offer. We bring students from different organizations to speak with the students. Activities are included in the event with the purpose of encouraging the students to seek a career in the STEM Field
