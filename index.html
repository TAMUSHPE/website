MentorSHPE Kickoff

Howdy SHPEsters! For those unaware of MentorSHPE, it is a program that can help you develop academically and professionally. Through upperclassmen serving as mentors and underclassmen learning under them as mentees; MentorSHPE is a great way to not only improve that GPA but also a to way gain valuable insight from one another. Below is the google docs link to sign up for MentorSHPE, (if you are interested in MentorSHPE and yet to sign up or yet to attend the first MentorSHPE event you may still do so):
