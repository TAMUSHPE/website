10th General Meeting

Howdy Ags!

Come join us for our tenth general meeting with BP & ALPFA! This will be a joint meeting paired up with ALPFA, the hispanic business organization on campus, as we host BP Oil & Gas. It will be a great opportunity to learn about BP and say hello to the business students. The event will be held this Wednesday, 3/8/17, 7:30PM at MEOB 301. Free food will be provided and most importantly the ATTIRE IS BUSINESS CASUAL. So make sure to dress appropriately. Hope to see all of you there! Thanks & Gig 'em!





Choosing Your Major Workshop

Howdy Ags!

This Thursday, 3/2/17, at 7:30PM in MEOB 301 we will be having a special Academic Development/MentorSHPE workshop dedicated into helping you decide on your engineering major of choice. There will be a wide variety of Academic advisors from the college of engineering which include ECEN, CIVIL, CHEN, and more presenting on the different majors. MentorSHPE pairs make sure to attend as there will be a great icebreaker and time to meet with one another. As always, this is not only restricted to those in MentorSHPE but it is open to every SHPEster and their guests. Free Panda Express will be given out! So make sure to come out to this great event, see you all soon! Thanks & Gig 'em!





9th General Meeting

Howdy SHPEsters!

Come join us for our 9th General Meeting of this academic year! We will be hosting Dow Chemical, a premier multinational chemical company. They will be presenting about their company, giving career advice, and Q&A will be available towards the end. The meeting will be this Wednesday, 2/22/17,b> at the Richardson Petroleum Building room 106b> starting at 7:30PM. Free food will be provided as always; hope to see all of you SHPEsters there! Thanks & Gig 'em!





8th General Meeting

Howdy SHPEsters!

Come join us for our 8th General Meeting of the school year! SHPE Alumni will be present as they speak about their companies, experiences, and how SHPE has impacted their career paths. It should be a great time to network and meet many people who were at some point in our shoes. As always, free food will be provided. The event will start at 7:30PM in the Richardson Petroleum Engineering Building room 106. Don't miss out!





Dinner Date Auction

Howdy SHPEsters!

Howdy Ags, come join us for our annual Dinner Date Auction. This is a fun way to find that special date for the month of February! You could sign up to be auctioned off by using this link, http://bit.do/shpedinnerdate. You must be signed into your tamu email in order for the link to work. The link asks you basic questions in order for your potential date to get an idea of whether you are their type or not. All SHPEsters and Aggies are welcome to sign up! Be sure to stop by RICH 101 (the Petroleum Engineering Building) this Friday February 3rd at 7:00 pm Good luck on finding those dates! T's and G's!

See y'all there!
