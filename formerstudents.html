ScholarSHPE Information

To become a sponsor to our organization, please fill out the Corporate Contribution Form found in our SponsorSHPE Proposal. Then, email the completed form to tamushpe@gmail.com addressed to our Vice President, Bryan Calleros, with "Completed CCF" in the subject line. We will then be in contact to see what events your company can host. We look forward to seeing how your company changes tamuSHPE! For more information, please don't hesitate to ask.

Join us for Events!

Stay connected with the students of our organizaion by joining us for various events. Attend our tailgate in the fall and root for our Aggies while chatting with our members. Or meet us at National Convention in Cleveland, OH this November! If you want an athletic event, participate in Lonestar Showdown where we play soccer or football against other chapters across the state. If food is more your style, enjoy some crawfish at our annual Crawfish Boil held in the Spring. We look forward to seeing what our Former Students have been up too!
