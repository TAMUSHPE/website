SHPE Former Students Tailgate

The moment you have all been waiting for.....ALL SHPEtinas and SHPEtinos clear your schedule for Saturday, September 15th because TAMU SHPE will be hosting a Former Students Tailgate! We want all former students to have a chance to reunite and join in with the current SHPE members for a "memorable" experience as the Aggies take on LA-Monroe. There will be great music, great games, and even greater people! Come out and join us as we're excited to welcome you back home to Aggieland!! Please RSVP on our Facebook Event Page and check out our supply list to see how you can help make this happen!
