Membership

Are you interested in becoming a member of SHPE?

1. Fill out the following form and email it to us at tamushpe@gmail.com OR submit it to the SHPE Office in EABB 106: TAMU SHPE Membership Form

TAMU Chapter dues: $5

SHPE National dues: $10

Total dues: $15

2. Follow the instructions below to be a member of SHPE National too!

Don't forget to apply online too!

Click the following link in order to continue with your application process: Online Application


1. Go to Join/Renew Membership, make sure to select Region 5 and Texas A&M as your chapter

2. Fill out your Account Information, and make sure that the Educational Information given to you is correct

3. Agree to the Code of Ethics and add the Membership to your cart

4. Pay for the membership and you should be set!

5. PLEASE REMEMBER TO SEND A SCREENSHOT OF YOUR PAYMENT CONFIRMATION TO secretary@shpe.tamu.edu!



Are you a returning member?

If you are a returning member and haven't renewed your membership, click on the following link in order to complete the application: TAMU SHPE Membership Form

Make sure you bring application and dues to the SHPE Office in EABB 106. Also, don't forget to apply online as well! Follow the directions above to become a member of SHPE National. YOU ARE NOT CONSIDERED A FULL MEMBER UNTIL BOTH APPLICATION PROCESES ARE COMPLETED.


Benefits for Becoming a Member

  • Internship, fellowships & co-op opportunities

  • Professional, career, & leadership training to give you a competitive advantage via
    • SHPE Conference
    • National Institutue of Leadership Advancement
    • Regional Leadership Development Conferences
  • Competition opportunities to test and refine your academic technical talent
  • GRE/GRAD Lab to guide you through the graduate school application process
  • Networking opportunities with high profile professionals, fellow students, corporate respresentatives and SHPE leaders
  • Discounted registration to SHPE National, Regional & Local events
  • Complimentary subscribtions to SHPE magazine and Hispanic Business Magazine
  • Complimentary SHPE stoles upon graduation
  • Access to OneSHPE portal 24/7 at www.shpe.org/oneshpe
  • Resume database that connects you with the nation's top employers
  • Access to view job postings by SHPE corporate companies on OneSHPE
  • Eligible to nominate or be nominated for SHPE's most prestigious national awards
