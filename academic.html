MentorSHPE

This program allows freshmen and sophomores to be paired with upperclassmen, in order to facilitate their transition into Texas A&M. Students receive a support system and someone they can go to if they have any questions. Participants are paired up by major and industry interests.

MentorSHPE Application

Professional MentorSHPE

This program allows juniors and seniors to be paired with engineering professionals, in order to facilitate their transition into industry after graduation. Students can receive support in job-searching and graduate school. Participants are paired up by major and industry interests.

SHPE Study Hours

Members gather on Discord every Monday from 5pm to 9pm to study together. There will be multiple channels available at study hours for members to work in groups. Study hours encourage members to dedicate a significant amount of time to studying and applying techniques learned from academic programs offered by the Office of Academic Development, in order to master their classes and be academically successful.

Academic Workshops

Academic workshops will be held throughout the year to help our members expand their knowledge in several areas. Among the workshops there is programming and coding, entry to a major, graduate school and the study abroad workshop.
