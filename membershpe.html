How do I become a member of the #SHPEFamilia?

You must complete BOTH the NATIONAL and TAMU CHAPTER application and pay their respective dues. Instructions on how to complete both are down below:

#TAMUSHPE Registration

  • Fill out the following Google Doc: Click Here
  • Pay $16 to cover your Chapter Dues (includes your very own #TAMUSHPE t-shirt) Here
  • Continue below to complete the registration for SHPE National

SHPE National Registration

  • Go to the following link to complete the registration: Click Here
  • Go to "Join/Renew Membership", make sure to select Region 5 and Texas A&M University as your chapter
  • Fill out your Account Information and make sure that the Educational Information given to you is correct
  • Agree to the Code of Ethics and add the Membership to your cart
  • Pay for the membership
  • PLEASE MAKE SURE TO SEND A SCREENSHOT OF YOUR PAYMENT CONFIRMATION TO secretary@shpe.tamu.edu
